About Fred Neumann

  4. Professional communication skills include fluency in English and Portuguese and also advanced Spanish speaking and writing skills
  5. Demonstrated ability securing customer loyalty and forging strong relationships with brands and audiences
  6. Computer skills include Google Tools, MS Office, HTML5, CSS, Hootsuite, Photoshop

as well as many other skills.
