About Zoë Adesina

Zoë Adesina is an Interactive Media Management Student at Centennial College in Toronto, Ontario. She has a background in digital media marketing and youth & community engagement and she is based in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).


Education & Background

Zoë studied at the University of Toronto Mississauga and received a B.Comm in Management and Economics in 2014. Her volunteer and work experiences have ranged from small non-profit organizations to large international corporations and she now possesses a vast skill set which include:

  1. Event production & management
  2. Public speaking & presentaion skills
  3. Digital & traditional marketing skills
  4. Design & graphic skills
  5. Team management & interpersonal skills
  6. Time-managment & organizational skills
  7. Problem-solving

as well as many other skills.

Passion & Interests

Zoë has a passion for youth & the arts. She believes strongly in the transformative effects of youth empowerment in local communities and youth engagement in national and global discussions. Zoë also is passionate and performing and visual arts as a medium for cultural expression, societtal commentary and satire
